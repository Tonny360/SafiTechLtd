Frequently Asked Questions

What painting services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of painting services including interior and exterior painting for residential and commercial properties, decorative finishes, cabinet refinishing, deck and fence staining, and color consultation services. Our team of professionals uses high-quality materials to ensure long-lasting results.

How do I choose the right paint for my project?

Choosing the right paint depends on several factors including the surface you're painting, the environment (interior vs. exterior), desired finish (matte, eggshell, satin, semi-gloss, or high-gloss), and your budget. Our paint experts can provide personalized recommendations based on your specific needs during a consultation.

Do you offer color consultation services?

Yes, we offer professional color consultation services to help you select the perfect colors for your space. Our color consultants consider factors like lighting, existing décor, architectural features, and your personal preferences to create a cohesive color scheme that enhances your property's aesthetic appeal.

How long does a typical painting project take?

The duration of a painting project varies based on the size and complexity of the job. A single room might take 1-2 days, while a full interior home painting could take 3-7 days. Exterior projects typically take 3-5 days depending on weather conditions and home size. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during the estimation process.

Do you provide warranties on your painting services?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. Our standard warranty covers labor and materials for 2 years on interior projects and 3 years on exterior projects. This warranty protects against peeling, blistering, and excessive fading. Extended warranty options are also available for select premium paint products.
